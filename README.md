# MediRide

MediRide is a medical transportation platform designed to provide fast booking, driver dispatch, patient tracking, and secure service management. The goal is to streamline non-emergency medical transport through a modern, reliable, and easy-to-use system.

## Features

- **Fast Booking**  
  Quick and simple ride scheduling for patients and caregivers.

- **Driver Dispatch**  
  Efficient driver assignment and real-time updates.

- **Patient Tracking**  
  Live tracking to ensure safety and transparency throughout the ride.

- **Secure Service Management**  
  Built with privacy and reliability in mind for medical transport operations.

## Technology

This repository contains the foundation for the MediRide platform, including configuration, documentation, and future development modules.
